About Me

Executive who has led large scale initiatives, focused on the development of digital strategies and products that transform businesses. Extensive experience bridging the gap between technology and business leaders to deliver on organizational and financial objectives. Skilled in designing user-centric platforms and experiences, program management, business process re-engineering, operational modeling, automation and journey mapping. Currently applying agile framework and scrum.


In 2015, joined the JPMorgan Chase executive team responsible for developing top talent. Architect of the flagship initiative, Leadership Edge, that leverages emerging technologies to deliver world-class leadership programming to 40,000 managers globally. Currently on the Cloud leadership team dedicated to the agile redesign of the employee experience and implementation of HCM Oracle Cloud globally to 250,000 employees.


Ten years at Goldman Sachs, developed custom and implemented vendor platforms, designed employee centric experiences and led numerous transformation programs – learning and development, talent, campus and experience hire recruiting, diversity and employee relations, HR operations off and on sourcing. Managed center of excellence with over 150 team members in multiple locations. Angeli lives in Maplewood, NJ with her husband Nick and kids Olivia and Henry. She's worked on Wall Street her whole career and managed HR tech prodjects. Her passion is product web development.
